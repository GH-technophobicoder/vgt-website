Video Game Therapy is your new portal for all things modern-classic in gaming. Modern and classic, you ask? Yep!

We at VGT are focused on bringing you bespoke content in relation to Video games from the 6th generation (Dreamcast, GCN, PS2 & Xbox), a time not too distant to be in memory yet still grants us the bona-fide comfort of digging into our nostalgic past!

Not only do we focus on the modern-classics, we’ll also write up reviews for the latest remakes and remasters - games that are revamped to modern tastes yet respects what made the originals so great to begin with.
